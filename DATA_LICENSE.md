# Catalog data license

Catalog records created by this project and explicitly included in a release manifest as
`CC0-1.0` are dedicated to the public domain under the
[CC0 1.0 Universal Public Domain Dedication](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/).

This dedication applies only to rights held by the contributor or project. It does not erase or
relicense third-party copyrights, database rights, benchmark terms, model licenses, trademarks,
privacy rights, or contractual restrictions.

Every released record must therefore identify:

- its contributor or authoritative source;
- the applicable source and data terms;
- whether redistribution has been verified;
- any attribution that must survive normalization;
- the evidence and review dates.

Records with unresolved or incompatible rights may appear in private research reports or source
review notes, but they must not appear in CC0 catalog snapshots.
